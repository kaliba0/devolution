How to buy a product ?

To begin with, you will necessary need a Discord Account. If you don't have any Discord Account, create one by cliquing here

If you have already one, log in to your Discord Account.

Once in your account, you will have to turn your account into the 'Developper Mode'. Follow these steps to do it :

  1. On the bottom left corner, there are some informations about you.
  2. Click on the setting wheel to open yout account settings. Once there, select the 'Advanced' section in the panel on your left and turn on the option 'Developper Mode'

Then, you need to copy your User ID. This is the ID you have to give when you buy a product :

Go back to the home page. In the informations about you on the bottom left corner, click on your name.

A menu should appear. There, copy your ID by cliquing on the 'Copy User ID' button
